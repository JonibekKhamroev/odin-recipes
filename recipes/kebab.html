Kebab

Description

One cannot imagine Uzbekistan without sunny flatbread, hot pilaf and, of course, without a delicious shish kebab

Smoke that is soaked in the aroma of fried meat on the coals goes up from the barbecue, appealing for gourmets.

Ingredients

  • Beef or Lamb
  • Broadtail Fat
  • Onions
  • Coriander
  • Spices

Steps

  1. The first step is to cut off the meat veins, cut it into slices across the fibers.
  2. Next, the meat is slightly beaten off and put in a bowl (in which there is a marinade).
  3. The next step is to prepare the marinating. To do this, grind the spices and salt in a mortar, cut the onion. All this is added to the dishes to the meat and pour carbonated water. Leave the meat in the marinade for an hour.
  4. Now cut the fat tail, and begin to string it on a skewer, alternating with pieces of meat. It remains to light a fire and fry the meat at an average temperature.
